About Me

I am Ankit Mohan Pandey, a Data Engineer with hands-on experience in designing, building, and maintaining scalable, reliable data platforms on Google Cloud Platform.

I work primarily on batch and streaming data pipelines, focusing on performance, data quality, and system reliability. My day-to-day work involves building pipelines using Apache Beam, orchestrating workflows with Cloud Composer (Airflow), and designing analytics-ready data models in BigQuery.

I enjoy working on problems where data volume, correctness, and latency matter, and I strongly believe in building systems that are simple, observable, and easy to operate at scale.

Beyond implementation, I actively focus on system design trade-offs, writing clean and maintainable code, improving pipeline reliability, and continuously learning modern data engineering practices.

This website is my space to document learnings, share technical insights, and explain complex data engineering concepts in a simple and practical way.
